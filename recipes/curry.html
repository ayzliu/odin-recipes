Chicken Curry

Description

This chicken curry is a flavorful delight, rich with spices and tender chicken pieces, perfect for a cozy dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cut into pieces
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 can coconut milk

Steps

  1. Season the chicken with salt, pepper, and curry powder.
  2. In a large pot, brown the chicken pieces. Remove and set aside.
  3. Add onions and garlic to the pot; cook until soft. Return the chicken to the pot, add coconut milk, and simmer until cooked through.
