Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is a comforting classic, layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melty cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles until al dente.
  3. Layer the noodles, meat sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
